Privacy Policy for Baby Girl's Calorie Tracker

Last updated: April 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in Baby Girl's Calorie Tracker ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with calorie tracking and fitness insights, the App may collect the following information:

  • Personal Data: such as age, weight, height, and gender, which you enter manually to calculate caloric needs.
  • Usage Data: food entries, nutritional information, and daily activities logged within the app.
  • Health App Integration: If you grant permission, the App may read from or write data to the Apple Health App (HealthKit). We do not use HealthKit data for marketing or advertising purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely to provide and improve the App's core functionality—helping you track your nutrition and fitness goals. All data is processed locally on your device and is not sold to third parties.

3. Data Retention and Security

We value your trust in providing your information, and we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. Your data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide you with the App's services. If you delete the App, your locally stored data will be deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not share your personal or health data with third-party networks for advertising.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
